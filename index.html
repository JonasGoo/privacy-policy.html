Privacy Policy
01

Data Minimization & Privacy

AI vs AI – Double Check is built on the principle of data privacy. I do not require any user accounts, and I do not collect, store, or sell any personal information such as names, email addresses, or locations.

02

Anonymous AI Processing (OpenRouter Gateway)

To provide the comparison feature, your queries are transmitted to OpenRouter. OpenRouter acts as a secure, anonymous gateway that routes your request to different AI models (e.g., GPT-4o mini, Gemini Flash, or Claude).

All queries are sent without personal identifiers. Your data is subject to the privacy policies of OpenRouter and the underlying model providers (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, etc.).

03

Local Device Storage

All chat history, settings, and token balances are stored exclusively on your local device. I have no access to your private conversations. You can delete this data at any time by removing the app from your device.

04

Quality Reporting (FormSubmit)

If you flag a response as inappropriate or incorrect, the specific AI output and your query are sent to me via FormSubmit.co. This is used solely to audit and improve the AI filtering and accuracy.

05

Advertising

I use Google AdMob to provide rewarded video ads. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve personalized ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Terms of Service
01

The “Double Check” Methodology

This app is specifically designed to reduce the risk of AI Hallucinations (false information generated by AI) by cross-referencing answers from multiple independent models. While this multi-stage verification significantly increases the reliability of the information, it does not guarantee 100% accuracy.

02

No Professional Advice

The outputs provided by AI 1, AI 2, or the Judge AI are for informational and educational purposes only. Do not use this app for medical, legal, financial, or any safety-critical decisions.

03

Prohibited Use

You agree not to use the app to generate harmful, illegal, or discriminatory content. I provide a reporting tool to flag such outputs, and I actively monitor these reports to improve the safety filters.

04
